Hen Party Ideas in Calgary

Calgary has more than enough to make a bachelorette weekend genuinely great.

6 min read · By the Mixler Team · Updated March 2026

The hen party or bachelorette in Calgary can go in a lot of different directions depending on the bride. This guide covers the formats that work best for different personality types and group compositions, from low-key afternoon events to full weekend itineraries.

Creative Hen Activities

Creative Hen Party Experiences in Calgary

Private cocktail-making classes are among the most popular hen party formats in Calgary. The format works for groups of 8-20, it's interactive and social without being physically demanding, and you're drinking what you make. Multiple Calgary providers including Mixler can arrange private cocktail events for hen groups.

Painting nights, pottery parties, and candle-making workshops all work as hen party activities because they're inclusive, creative, and produce a take-home keepsake. The bridal party format for pottery can be particularly sweet: everyone throws a small bowl or vase, and the bride's piece gets a prominent place on the shelf.

Keepsake tip

Activities where everyone makes something tangible (pottery, candles, painting) produce natural hen party keepsakes that are more personal than merchandise.

Active Hen Party Activities in Calgary

Food and Drink Hen Events

Food and Drink Hen Party Ideas

A chef-led cooking event or food-themed workshop is one of the best hen party formats for groups that love food. Sushi-making, pasta nights, charcuterie board building, and cocktail pairings are all available in Calgary through Mixler and other providers.

Wine tasting events and whisky-pairing evenings work particularly well for brides who prefer a more sophisticated afternoon format. Some Calgary wine bars offer private group seatings with a sommelier host, which can be arranged as a dedicated hen afternoon.

Format flexibility

Mixler can combine a creative activity (cocktail-making, charcuterie) with a social game element (trivia, murder mystery) for hen groups that want more variety.

Planning Tips

How to Plan a Hen Party in Calgary

Give yourself 4-6 weeks lead time for private hen party bookings. Popular formats in summer (June-August) fill even faster. Most private event providers need a confirmed headcount and a deposit to hold your date.

Think about the full day arc. A great Calgary hen day might look like: afternoon bridal brunch in Inglewood, 3pm cocktail-making class, dinner reservation at a private room in a restaurant, and evening dancing or a bar crawl on 17th Ave. Having the bookable middle anchor (the class or workshop) makes the rest of the day easy to plan around.
