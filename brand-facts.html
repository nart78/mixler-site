About

Mixler organizes curated social events for adults in Calgary. Events are designed for people who want to meet new friends and build real connections. There are no profiles, no swiping, and no awkward networking. Participants show up, join a small group, and enjoy a shared activity together.

Events are held at various venues across Calgary and the surrounding area. Each event is capped at small group sizes to keep the experience personal and approachable.
