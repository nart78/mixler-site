Bath Bomb Making in Calgary

Mix, mold, and take home bath bombs you actually made yourself.

No Experience Needed Groups Welcome All Skill Levels

Bath bomb making workshops are one of those activities that sounds like a nice idea and turns out to be genuinely great. You get to choose your scents, colours, and add-ins, then mix and mold them into bath bombs you take home. In Calgary, these workshops have become popular for groups, bachelorettes, and solo attendees who just want a creative, sensory, and social evening without anything too intense.

What It's Like

What to Expect

🛁

All materials provided

The instructor supplies baking soda, citric acid, oils, fragrances, colours, and molds. You don't need to bring anything except your scent preferences.

🌸

Choose your own scents and colours

Most workshops offer a selection of essential oils, fragrance blends, and colorants so you can customize your bath bombs entirely to your taste.

🧪

Learn the chemistry

A good instructor explains why bath bombs fizz, what makes them skin-safe, and how to adjust recipes for different effects. The science makes it more interesting.

🎁

Take home what you make

You leave with a batch of bath bombs packaged to take home or give as gifts. Most workshops yield between four and eight bombs per person.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Bath Bomb Making Event

  • 1 Work quickly when mixing. Once liquid touches the dry ingredients, the fizzing reaction starts and you need to get into the mold before it activates too far.
  • 2 Less liquid colourant is more. Too much can stain your bathtub. Stick to the amounts the instructor recommends.
  • 3 Choose complementary scents if you're mixing essential oils. Citrus and mint work well together. Lavender and eucalyptus are a classic combination.
  • 4 Let your bombs harden completely before removing them from the mold. Impatience leads to crumbling, not from baking experience, just physics.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need any experience to make bath bombs? +
None at all. The instructor walks you through every step and the process is straightforward enough for complete beginners to get great results.
Are the bath bombs safe for sensitive skin? +
Most workshops use skin-safe, cosmetic-grade ingredients. Let your instructor know about any sensitivities so they can recommend appropriate fragrance and colour options.
How many bath bombs will I make? +
Most Calgary bath bomb workshops yield four to eight bombs per person, depending on the mold sizes used. Enough to use some and gift some.
How long does a bath bomb workshop take? +
Typically 90 minutes to two hours, including mixing, molding, and waiting for the bombs to set enough to package safely.
