Wreath Making in Calgary

Make a seasonal wreath that you'll actually want to hang on your door.

All Skill Levels Groups Welcome No Experience Needed

Wreath making workshops in Calgary bring people together around one of the most satisfying creative projects you can finish in a single evening. You work with real greenery, dried flowers, ribbon, and seasonal accents. The process is part floral design, part craft, and the result is something that looks store-bought but is completely your own.

What It's Like

What to Expect

🌿

Fresh and dried materials

Workshops use a mix of fresh greenery, dried florals, and seasonal accents depending on the time of year.

🔧

Wire frame and technique

You'll learn how to secure materials to a wire frame using floral wire and picks. It's easier than it looks.

🎀

Finishing touches

Ribbon, picks, and decorative elements are added last. This is where your wreath really takes on its personality.

🏠

Ready to hang

Your wreath goes straight on your door. Most last weeks to months depending on the materials used.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Wreath Making Event

  • 1 Secure each bundle of greenery firmly before adding the next. Loose materials shift over time.
  • 2 Work in one direction around the frame for a consistent, full look.
  • 3 Calgary's seasons make wreath making especially popular in fall and winter. Seasonal workshops sell out fast.
  • 4 This is a great group activity. A table of friends making wreaths together is a genuinely good evening.
  • 5 Mixler tracks wreath making events in Calgary. Join the waitlist and we'll let you know as soon as one is added.
Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need any crafting experience? +
No experience needed. The instructor guides you through every step, from attaching greenery to adding the final ribbon.
What season is wreath making most popular? +
Fall and winter are the most popular times, but workshops happen year-round with seasonal materials.
How long does a wreath making workshop take? +
Most sessions are 2 to 3 hours. You'll have a completed wreath ready to hang by the end.
Can I customize my wreath? +
Yes. You choose your colour palette, ribbon, and accents. No two wreaths from the same workshop ever look alike.
