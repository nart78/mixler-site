What It's Like

What to Expect

✍️

Grip and posture first

The instructor starts with how to hold the pen and position your paper. Getting this right from the start makes everything else easier.

📐

Basic strokes before letters

You'll practice upstrokes, downstrokes, and ovals before forming full letters. It's the foundation of everything.

🖊️

Your choice of style

Most beginner classes cover modern calligraphy or brush lettering. The instructor will guide you based on the class focus.

📄

Practice sheets and paper included

All materials are provided. You take your practice sheets home so you can keep working on it.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Calligraphy Classes Event

  • 1 Relax your grip. Holding the pen too tightly is the single biggest thing that slows beginners down.
  • 2 Use smooth paper. Textured paper catches the nib and makes clean strokes harder to pull off.
  • 3 Go slow on the downstrokes. Speed is the enemy of even pressure and good lettering.
  • 4 Calligraphy is a genuinely useful skill in Calgary's wedding and events market. Once you have it, people ask for it.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need artistic ability to learn calligraphy? +
No. Calligraphy is a technical skill. If you can follow instruction and practice the strokes, you can learn it.
What style of calligraphy will I learn? +
Most beginner classes focus on modern calligraphy or brush lettering. Check the event listing for specifics.
What supplies are provided? +
Nibs, holders, ink, and practice paper are all provided. You take your sheets home to keep practicing.
How long does a calligraphy class take? +
Most sessions run 2 to 3 hours. That's enough time to learn the basics and leave with real muscle memory.
