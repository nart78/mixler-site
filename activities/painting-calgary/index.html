Painting Events in Calgary

Pick up a brush and paint something you're actually proud of.

All Skill Levels Groups Welcome No Experience Needed

Painting events in Calgary have exploded in popularity, and for good reason. Whether you're a total beginner or someone who dabbles on weekends, a guided painting session is one of the most satisfying ways to spend an evening. You follow along, chat with people around you, and walk away with something you made yourself.

What It's Like

What to Expect

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Painting Events Event

  • 1 Wear something you don't mind getting paint on, even if aprons are provided.
  • 2 Don't stress about making it perfect. The fun is in the process, not the result.
  • 3 Calgary winters are long, so a cozy paint night is a great way to get out of the house mid-week.
  • 4 Going with a group? Painting side by side makes for a great photo at the end of the night.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need any experience to join a painting event? +
Not at all. Painting events are designed for beginners. The host guides you through every step of the painting.
What should I wear? +
Wear clothes you're okay getting a little paint on. Aprons are usually provided, but accidents happen.
Can I come solo? +
Absolutely. Solo attendees are completely welcome. You'll be seated with other guests and it's easy to chat while you paint.
How long does a painting event usually last? +
Most sessions run 2 to 3 hours, which is enough time to finish a painting without feeling rushed.
