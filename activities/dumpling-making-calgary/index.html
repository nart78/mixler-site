Dumpling Making in Calgary

Make a batch of dumplings from scratch and eat every single one.

All Skill Levels Groups Welcome No Experience Needed

Dumpling making classes in Calgary are joyful, hands-on, and very delicious. You mix and roll the dough, prepare the filling, and practice the folding technique that makes each dumpling look like it was made with care. It's a skill rooted in tradition, and learning it from someone who knows it well makes the whole experience feel meaningful.

What It's Like

What to Expect

🥟

Dough from scratch

You'll mix and knead your own dumpling dough. It's simple but the texture matters, and the instructor shows you what to look for.

🧅

Filling preparation

Most classes use a pork and cabbage or vegetarian filling. You'll prep it, season it, and taste for balance.

👐

Folding technique

The folding is the tricky part and also the most satisfying. The instructor teaches you a classic pleat fold step by step.

🍳

Cooking methods

You'll pan-fry, steam, or boil your dumplings (sometimes all three) and taste the differences between each method.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Dumpling Making Event

  • 1 Don't overfill your wrappers. A small amount of filling is much easier to seal than a generous one.
  • 2 Keep your finished dumplings on a floured surface so they don't stick before cooking.
  • 3 Calgary has a great Chinese and East Asian food scene. Taking a dumpling class gives you a real appreciation for what you're eating when you go out.
  • 4 Make a big batch. Dumplings freeze beautifully and cook from frozen in minutes.
  • 5 Mixler lists dumpling making events in Calgary when they're added. Join the waitlist and we'll email you when one is scheduled.
Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need cooking experience? +
No. Dumpling making classes are designed for all skill levels. The instructor guides you through every step.
What type of dumplings will I make? +
Most classes focus on Chinese-style dumplings like jiaozi. The event listing will specify the style and filling.
Can I take extras home? +
Yes. Most classes produce more than you can eat. Extras are yours to take home and freeze.
Is there a vegetarian option? +
Many classes offer vegetarian filling options. Check with the organizer when booking if this is important to you.
