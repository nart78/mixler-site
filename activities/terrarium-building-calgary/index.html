Terrarium Building in Calgary

Build a little living world in a glass container and take it home.

All Skill Levels Great for Solo No Experience Needed

Terrarium building workshops in Calgary are a wonderfully satisfying way to spend an evening. You pick your plants, layer the soil and rocks, arrange moss and decorative elements, and end up with a self-contained miniature garden. It's creative, tactile, and the result actually lives on your desk or windowsill for months.

What It's Like

What to Expect

🌿

Choose your plants

Workshops offer a selection of small plants, succulents, or tropicals. You pick what goes into your container.

🪨

Layer by layer assembly

You'll learn the proper layering of gravel, activated charcoal, and soil so your terrarium drains and breathes properly.

🔮

Glass containers provided

The vessel is included. Most workshops offer a range of shapes and sizes for you to choose from.

🌱

Care instructions included

You'll leave knowing exactly how to water and light your terrarium so it stays healthy at home.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Terrarium Building Event

  • 1 Don't overwater. Terrariums need far less water than you think, especially closed ones.
  • 2 Use long tweezers or a chopstick to position small plants in narrow containers. Your fingers often can't reach.
  • 3 Calgary's dry indoor air in winter makes closed terrariums a great option. They self-regulate humidity.
  • 4 A terrarium makes a great long-lasting gift. Many people make extras at workshops for friends.
  • 5 Mixler lists terrarium building events in Calgary when they're added. Join the waitlist and we'll send you a heads up.
Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to know anything about plants? +
No plant knowledge required. The instructor covers everything, including care instructions for after you take it home.
What type of terrarium will I make? +
It depends on the workshop. Some focus on succulents, others on tropical plants. The listing will tell you which type.
How do I keep it alive after the workshop? +
You'll get care instructions at the end of the session covering light, water, and maintenance. Most terrariums are low-maintenance.
Is the container included? +
Yes. The glass container is included in the workshop cost, along with all soil, plants, and decorative materials.
