Puppy Yoga in Calgary

Downward dog, but make it literal. Puppy yoga in Calgary is everything.

No Experience Needed Great for Solo All Skill Levels

Puppy yoga is exactly what it sounds like: a yoga class where rescue or foster puppies roam freely around the room while you try your best to hold poses. Calgary's puppy yoga events sell out quickly because the combination of gentle movement, stress relief, and unbearable puppy cuteness hits something genuinely therapeutic. You don't need yoga experience, and you will leave smiling.

What It's Like

What to Expect

🐶

Free-roaming puppies throughout the class

Puppies wander the room during the session. Some will climb on you, some will sleep on your mat, and some will demand your full attention at the worst possible moment.

🧘

Gentle, beginner-friendly yoga

The yoga taught at puppy events is deliberately accessible. Instructors understand that concentration is harder than usual and keep the sequences light and forgiving.

📸

Photo time before and after

Most events build in time before the class and after for photos with the puppies. This is not optional. You will take photos.

🐾

Often supports rescue organizations

Many Calgary puppy yoga events partner with local rescues or foster networks, meaning the puppies are looking for homes. Adoption happens more than you might expect.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Puppy Yoga Event

  • 1 Book early. Puppy yoga events in Calgary regularly sell out days or weeks in advance.
  • 2 Wear clothes you don't mind getting puppy paws and possibly puppy drool on. Your nicest activewear is the wrong choice here.
  • 3 Don't eat right before the class. It's gentle yoga but a full stomach is uncomfortable, and you'll be moving around and possibly on the floor a lot.
  • 4 Be gentle with the puppies and follow the handlers' guidance. Their comfort and safety is always the priority.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need yoga experience for puppy yoga? +
None at all. The yoga is designed to be easy and the puppies ensure no one is taking themselves too seriously. Total beginners are very welcome.
Are the puppies from a rescue? +
Many Calgary puppy yoga events partner with local rescue organizations or foster homes. Check the specific event details for information on where the puppies come from.
What should I bring? +
A yoga mat if you have one, though many events provide them. Water, comfortable clothes, and your phone for photos are the essentials.
Is puppy yoga suitable for people with allergies? +
That depends on your allergy severity. If you have dog allergies, a puppy yoga event may not be the right choice. Antihistamines help some people, but check with your doctor first.
