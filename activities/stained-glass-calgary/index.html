Stained Glass in Calgary

Cut, wrap, and solder your own stained glass piece in one session.

All Skill Levels No Experience Needed Great for Solo

Stained glass workshops in Calgary are one of those experiences that feels more advanced than it actually is. You cut glass, wrap the edges with copper foil, and solder the pieces together into a finished panel or suncatcher. The instructor makes each step approachable, and the result is something that genuinely glows when you hold it up to the light.

What It's Like

What to Expect

✂️

Glass cutting basics

You'll learn how to score and break glass cleanly. It feels nerve-wracking at first but becomes satisfying quickly.

🔧

Copper foiling technique

Each piece of glass is wrapped in copper foil tape before assembly. Precision here makes the soldering much cleaner.

🔥

Soldering it together

The instructor guides you through soldering all the pieces together. This is the most technically exciting part of the process.

🌈

You choose your colours

Glass comes in a range of colours and textures. Your colour choices shape the whole look of your finished piece.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Stained Glass Event

  • 1 Score in one smooth motion without stopping partway. Retracing a score line causes rough breaks.
  • 2 Keep your copper foil wrapping tight and even. Gaps make soldering harder and the joints look messy.
  • 3 Safety glasses are non-negotiable. Glass fragments are small and fast.
  • 4 Calgary has some talented stained glass artists. A workshop is a great way to appreciate the craft before buying pieces from local makers.
  • 5 Mixler lists stained glass events in Calgary when they come up. Join the waitlist and we'll let you know when one is scheduled.
Common Questions

FAQ

Is stained glass safe for beginners? +
Yes, with proper instruction. Safety glasses are required and the instructor covers every safety step before you handle any tools.
What will I make? +
Most beginner workshops have you make a small panel or suncatcher that you can hang in a window at home.
Do I take my piece home the same day? +
Yes. Stained glass pieces are complete by the end of the session. You take it home that night.
How long does a stained glass workshop take? +
Most beginner sessions run 3 hours or so. There are a lot of steps, and the instructor takes time with each one.
