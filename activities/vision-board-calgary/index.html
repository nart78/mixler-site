Vision Board Making in Calgary

Cut, glue, and get clear on what you actually want this year.

Great for Solo No Experience Needed Groups Welcome

Vision board workshops take the traditionally solo activity of goal visualization and make it a social event, which turns out to work really well. Calgary hosts these regularly at the start of the year and at various points throughout, bringing people together to cut magazines, print images, and assemble a visual representation of what they're working toward. The facilitated format and group energy help you get past the surface goals and into what actually matters.

What It's Like

What to Expect

✂️

Materials provided

Workshops supply magazines, scissors, glue, poster boards, stickers, and pens. You bring yourself and whatever clarity you already have about what you want.

🎯

Guided intention-setting

Facilitators walk you through reflection exercises before the crafting begins. This helps you move past generic goals and into more honest, specific intentions.

🖼️

A physical artifact to take home

You leave with a finished vision board you can hang somewhere visible. Having the goal in your environment genuinely influences behaviour over time.

🤝

A supportive, open social atmosphere

Vision board events attract people who are invested in their own growth, which makes the social dynamic noticeably positive. Sharing your board with the group is optional but often the highlight.

Insider Advice

Tips for Your First Vision Board Making Event

  • 1 Look through magazines for images and words that feel right before you know why. Your gut is doing useful work in the cutting phase.
  • 2 Don't limit your board to career and money goals. Physical health, relationships, experiences, and how you want to feel are all fair game.
  • 3 Hang your finished board somewhere you'll see it daily. On the inside of a wardrobe door or above a desk works well.
  • 4 Revisit your board mid-year. Some intentions evolve, some land ahead of schedule, and noticing the shift is valuable in itself.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to know my goals before a vision board workshop? +
No. Part of the process is helping you discover or clarify your intentions. Many people arrive unsure and leave with a surprisingly clear direction.
Are materials provided? +
Yes. Most Calgary vision board workshops supply everything: magazines, scissors, glue sticks, poster boards, pens, and stickers. Bringing your own favourite magazine is a nice addition.
When is the best time of year to do a vision board? +
January is popular, but transitions like birthdays, equinoxes, or any major life shift are equally valid timing. The best time is when you feel ready to be intentional.
How long do vision board workshops take? +
Most run two to three hours, including the reflection exercises, crafting time, and optional sharing at the end.
