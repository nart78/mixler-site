Whether you're looking for a chill evening or a high-energy night, we've got the vibe for you.

Social Mixers

Our signature events. Walk into a curated venue, grab a drink, and let our hosts guide the conversation. Icebreakers keep things flowing, small group sizes keep things personal. You'll leave with at least 5 new connections every time.

  • Curated venues across Calgary
  • Structured icebreakers (no awkward silence)
  • Groups of 30-40 for the perfect vibe
  • Complimentary welcome drink

Trivia & Drinks

Teams of strangers battle it out over five rounds of trivia at Calgary's best spots. You don't need to know everything. You just need to show up and have a good time. Winners get bragging rights and a round on us.

  • 5 rounds of themed trivia
  • Random team assignment (meet new people)
  • Prizes for top teams
  • Kensington's best venues

Speed Friending

Like speed dating, but for friendships. Rotate through short, fun conversations with a room full of interesting people. By the end of the night, you'll have met everyone and found your people. No pressure, just real connections.

  • 5-minute rotating conversations
  • Guided prompts (deeper than small talk)
  • Meet everyone in the room
  • Connection cards to exchange info
